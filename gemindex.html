The Gem Collector

Instructions:


Each time you click a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each gem is unknown until you click on it.

When the game starts, the values of each gem will be assigned randomly.

You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number.

You lose the game if your total score goes above the goal number.
